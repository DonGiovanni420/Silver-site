# SEO Research & Keyword Notes

Goal: Target low-to-moderate competition keywords with clear intent.
Focus on educational and beginner-friendly searches.

---

## Core High-Intent Keywords

| Keyword | Intent | Target Page | Notes |
|------|------|------|------|
| what is silver bullion | informational | Education hub | Beginner entry point |
| silver spot price | informational | Spot Price page | High traffic, update often |
| how to buy silver | transactional | Buy Silver guide | Strong affiliate potential |
| best places to buy silver | transactional | Buy Silver comparison | Conversion-focused |
| silver coins vs bars | comparison | Buying guide | Mid-funnel |
| what is junk silver | informational | Education article | Good internal linking |
| is silver a good investment | informational | Education article | Avoid hype language |
| how to sell silver | informational | Sell Silver guide | Trust building |
| silver price today | informational | Spot Price page | Search variation |

---

## Keyword Mapping Rules

- One **primary keyword** per page
- 2–4 secondary variations max
- Do not target the same primary keyword on multiple pages
- Educational intent first, sales second

---

## Content Intent Breakdown

### Informational
- Definitions
- Explanations
- Market basics
- Trust builders

### Transactional
- Buying guides
- Dealer comparisons
- Product-type explanations

### Comparison
- Coins vs bars
- Online vs local dealers
- Silver vs gold (later)

---

## On-Page SEO Rules (Simple)

- Keyword in:
  - Page title
    - H1
      - First 100 words (naturally)
      - Clear subheadings (H2/H3)
      - Internal links to:
        - Education hub
          - Buying guide (if relevant)
          - No keyword stuffing
