

# Monetization Strategy

## Primary Revenue Methods
- Affiliate links to reputable silver dealers
- Educational content that naturally leads to buying decisions

## What We Do NOT Do
- No urgency or manipulation
- No "silver is about to explode" language
- No paid promotions without disclosure

## Conversion Points
- Buy Silver guides
- Comparison articles
- Educational pages with contextual links

## Affiliate Link Rules
- Links appear after education, not before
- One primary CTA per page
- Educational pages with contextual links

## Trust Principles
- Explain risks and downsides honestly
- Avoid price predictions
- Focus on long-term understanding

## Future Monetization (Later)
- Email list (market updates / guides)
- Simple calculators or tools
- Display ads only if traffic supports it
